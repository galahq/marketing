MSC accepts proposals on a rolling basis. The MSC curricular advisors meet at least once per quarter to review proposals. Therefore, your proposal will be reviewed within three months of submission. We will notify you if your proposal is selected. (link: action/make/production-guide/process/funding-disbursement#content text:Funding is available) for selected proposals. 

You can review (link: https://umich.box.com/shared/static/0tartibmqdj2ut2e330619z1gy0l8dw5.pdf text: this rubric that we use to evaluate the proposals). Please submit your proposal using the button below.

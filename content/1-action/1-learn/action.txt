Title: Learn

----

Tagline: Multi-media engagement for all learners; practical skills for real problems.

----

Description: Each MSC tells a story. First, a real-world based problem with a challenging decision. Then, the perspectives from key decision maker and main stakeholders. These are accompanied by audiovisual sources that provide diverse and in-depth insights to the case. Lastly, interactive learning exercises for students to analyze priorities and tradeoffs. MSCs provide an immersive and exploratory educational approach to teach practical skills in environmental sustainability.

----

##Real

The interactive learning exercises provide a vessel to fully immerse yourself in an environment where you are able to apply your knowledge and come to real life decisions.

##Understandable
The podcast allows you an insider view by listening to direct conversations between stakeholders and practitioners.
The multimedia edgenotes give you outside perspectives about the case at hand.

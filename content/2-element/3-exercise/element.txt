Text: 

Interactive learning exercises are activities that enhance students’ mastery of specific tools and wider skills. They provide a toolkit of quantitative, technical, analytical and creative competencies. And foster an environment for students to practice collaboration, self agency and intercultural communication.

Interactive experience with case material that allows students to dive deeper. 
Hands on. 
Provides an opportunity for students to understand the implications that this case carries with it. 

##Types
role play
field exercise
analysis
design 

##Examples
a role play that asks students to reflect on views of various stakeholders
the application of economic or social cost-benefit analysis
an assignment to develop a digital media skills, such as write a blog or tweet, and track their impact
a project to build part of a prototype, like solar installations or straw bale construction

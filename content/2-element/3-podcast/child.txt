Text: 

MSC podcasts present the material in the form of a lively conversation. Providing an opportunity to untangle case narratives and assumptions and develop potential solutions, the podcasts engage stakeholders, experts and decision-makers. Through a free form discussion driven approach, our podcasts shed light on underexplored aspects of the case material. 

Here are some clips pulled from existing MSC podcast to give you a taste of how MSC podcasts: 

##Some Heading

#####Style
###Some Heading
Lasting about 30 minutes to an hour, MSC podcasts often take advantage of the great music selection at the University of Michigan’s student run radio station, WCBN FM. 

#####Topics
###Some Heading
MSC podcast features discussion about the background of the case, conversation about key concepts or methods, dialogue that untangles challenges and assumptions, debate over objectives and tradeoffs.

Connects experts and citizens, bridging research and practice.
